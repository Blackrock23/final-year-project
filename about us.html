About Us

Rajorshi Basu is the creator of Studio Durga, the first Indian anime studio. The studio's first film, Karmachakra, was released in 2020

Karmachakra is an anime-influenced mystery drama film set in India. It was produced by Studio Durga, which was founded in 2016. The film was made with a small team of four people and took four years to complete. Basu's goal was to create homegrown animation that's not aimed at children, and that looks like it came from Japan. The film's title translates to "Wheel of Fate". It was billed as the first independent Indian anime production. A 20-minute pilot segment was released on YouTube in February 2020.
